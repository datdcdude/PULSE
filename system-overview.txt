System Overview

Pulse Protocol is composed of four main subsystems:
- Tier Pools  
- Apex Cycles  
- PulsePass Governance  
- DAO-Managed Treasury  

Participants enter Bronze and climb through the ecosystem until they mint a PulsePass.

4.1 Components
- $PULSE Token – Governance + utility  
- Tier Pools – Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum  
- Apex Cycle – Fulfilment of 8-unit tiers  
- PulsePass – Governance NFT-equivalent (non-financial)  
- Treasury – DAO-controlled sustainability engine  
- Pulse Protocol DAO** – Community governance body  

4.2 Participant Lifecycle

1. Enter Bronze  
2. Progress sequentially up the tiers  
3. Complete Apex cycles  
4. Reach Platinum  
5. Mint PulsePass  
6. Gain voting rights  
7. Maintain optional perpetual placement in all tiers
